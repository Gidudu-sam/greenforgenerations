Welcome to Green for Generations Volunteering

Green For Generations Volunteers are dedicated to promoting environmental conservation and sustainability through volunteering. Our efforts focus on protecting natural ecosystems, promote sustainability practices, and raise awareness about environmental issues. Through initiatives such as tree restoration, reducing waste, advocating for renewable energy, and community engagement, we aim to create a greener and more sustainable future in the Emirates.

Conservation & restoration

Preserving, restoring natural ecosystems to maintain biodiversity and eco-balance.

Volunteering

Inspiring and empowering individuals to make a meaningful impact through volunteering.

Sustainability

Driving sustainable practices that protect our planet, empowering communities, and ensure a thriving future for generations to come.

The Story Behind Our Movement

We are a passionate youth-led environmental organisation founded by dedicated UAE volunteers with the aim of promoting environment conservation, mitigate climate change, support small sustainable businesses, boosting local agriculture products in UAE and Africa. Through hands-on volunteer work, awareness campaigns, and partnerships, we aim to create lasting environmental and social impact while fostering global sustainability efforts.
