Who We Are

Welcome to Green Generation Volunteers

Founded by Uganda volunteers based in the UAE — driven by passion for the environment.

We are a youth-led environmental organisation dedicated to tree planting, lake shore clean ups, and climate action — empowering communities to build a greener future in Uganda and Africa.

The Story Behind Our Movement

We are a passionate youth-led environmental organisation founded by dedicated Uganda volunteers with the aim of promoting environment conservation, mitigate climate change, support small sustainable businesses, boosting local agriculture products in Uganda and Africa. Through hands-on volunteer work, awareness campaigns, and partnerships, we aim to create lasting environmental and social impact while fostering global sustainability efforts.
